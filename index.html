Two unordered facts about Gojo

  • Limitless Cursed Technique: Gojo Satoru possesses the powerful "Limitless" cursed technique, which is derived from the Gojo family. This technique grants him control over space at an atomic level, allowing him to manipulate distances and create a barrier that stops any physical attacks. One of the most notable aspects of this technique is "Infinity," which creates an infinite distance between Gojo and any attack, effectively making him untouchable.
  • Six Eyes: Gojo's unique ability, known as the "Six Eyes," greatly enhances his perception and usage of cursed energy. The Six Eyes provide him with extraordinary vision, allowing him to see cursed energy with incredible detail and precision. This ability also reduces the amount of cursed energy he uses, making him more efficient in battle. The combination of the Limitless technique and the Six Eyes makes Gojo one of the most powerful and formidable sorcerers in the "Jujutsu Kaisen" series.

Two ordered facts about Sukuna

  1. King of Curses: Ryomen Sukuna, also known simply as Sukuna, is a legendary cursed spirit referred to as the "King of Curses." He was once a human sorcerer who lived over a thousand years ago, known for his immense power and cruelty. After his death, Sukuna's power was so great that his body could not be completely destroyed, leading to his remains being divided into 20 indestructible fingers that became powerful cursed objects.
  2. Possession of Yuji Itadori: In the "Jujutsu Kaisen" series, Sukuna's influence resurfaces when Yuji Itadori consumes one of Sukuna's fingers, becoming his vessel. Despite Sukuna's attempts to take control of Yuji's body, Yuji manages to suppress Sukuna's influence most of the time. However, when Sukuna does take over, his power and abilities become accessible, making Yuji incredibly formidable but also posing a significant threat to those around him.
